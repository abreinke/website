Artist Background

Alicia Breinke is a Sacramemento-based artist with over seven years of experience in digital photography and graphic design. She currently holds an A.A. degree from American River College and a B.F.A. degree from San Jose State University. Some of Breinke's works have been featured in magazine publications and art galleries. She plans to continue creating digital collages and photographing natural objects, but she hopes to develop her skill in digital video, interactive code, and fine art.
